Check your Steam Library's Linux Availability

Enter your steam user ID to see which of your Steam games are Linux compatible!



Note: Allow 2-3 minutes for the list to be generated and filtered

How to Use

To get your Steam profile ID, go to the Steam Community page and search by your username and pull up your profile
[ http://SteamCommunity.com ]

The result will be something like:
http://steamcommunity.com/profiles/123456789

Copy the "123456789" and paste into the textbox and click on the "Check My Steam Library!" button


Security Info

We do not store any information in our database. We will never be store your searches or data, and the searches and data will never given/sold to a third party.

All data used to generate the report is publicly accessible through Steam's API and Steam Community, including your steam user ID. The report is generated on the fly by accessing the public API
